Deepmatcher

Business Objective

Life insurance models heavily rely upon third-party data fields (things like lifestyle information, etc). This data exists in a database with PII that needs to be joined to customer data.

Challenges

  • Nicknames vs full names
  • Mispellings in names
  • Initials vs full names

Objective

  • Develop a tool that allows automation of this process
  • Produces high quality matches
  • Easily accessible interface for users
